About Saksham Biosurgical Services

Founded in 2021, Saksham Biosurgical Services is a trusted provider of high-quality hospital equipment and comprehensive healthcare solutions. We specialize in supplying and servicing a wide range of medical systems, including Medical Gas Pipeline Systems (MGPS), critical care equipment, physiotherapy devices, operation theatre (OT) setups, hospital beds, and more. We also offer refurbished medical equipment, ensuring cost-effective options without compromising on performance or safety. Our mission is to equip healthcare facilities with reliable, efficient, and advanced technology that supports better patient care.

Medical Gas Pipeline Systems (MGPS)

Saksham Biosurgical Services specializes in designing, installing, and maintaining Medical Gas Pipeline Systems (MGPS) for hospitals and healthcare facilities. Our MGPS solutions are built with precision, ensuring safe, reliable, and continuous supply of medical gases like Oxygen, Nitrous Oxide, Compressed Air, and Vacuum.

  • Centralized Oxygen Supply Systems
  • Vacuum and Suction Units
  • Compressed Air & Nitrous Oxide Systems
  • Bed Head Panels & Gas Outlets
  • Alarm and Monitoring Systems

With expert engineering and a dedicated service team, we ensure every MGPS project meets international safety standards and provides reliable infrastructure for critical patient care.
